2. **Real Footage:** Actual CCTV scenarios and AI detection
3. **Screen Recordings:** Live Safe City software interface
4. **Mobile Mockups:** Realistic iPhone/Android demonstrations
5. **Animated Graphics:** Smooth transitions and modern effects
6. **Brand Consistency:** Purple/blue gradient theme throughout

### Audio Production:
- **Professional Hindi Voiceover:** Clear, confident, engaging tone
- **Background Music:** Modern tech-inspired instrumental (royalty-free)
- **Sound Effects:** UI clicks, notification sounds, alert tones
- **Audio Levels:** Balanced mix with clear voiceover priority

- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)

- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Bitrate:** 8-10 Mbps for high quality
