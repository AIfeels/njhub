Challenge

Solution

We implemented our Safe City software with AI-powered threat detection, fire safety monitoring, and real-time alert systems. The solution included mobile app integration for security staff and automated incident reporting.

Within 6 months of implementation, the complex saw dramatic improvements in security effectiveness, faster incident response, and enhanced visitor safety. The ROI was achieved in just 8 months.
